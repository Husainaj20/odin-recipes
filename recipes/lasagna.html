Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté garlic and onion until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, followed by noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  8. Top with remaining meat sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
