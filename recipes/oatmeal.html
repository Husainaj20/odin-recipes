Oatmeal Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups water or milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional toppings: fruits, nuts, honey, or cinnamon

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring water or milk to a boil.
  2. Add a pinch of salt and stir in the oats.
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Remove from heat and let it sit for a minute.
  5. Serve hot with your favorite toppings.
